# Insights & Interpretation
•	Order fulfillment is highly reliable since the most common order status is “Shipped,” with no cancellations or delays.

•	2018 was the strongest year in both revenue and profit and had the highest product order volume. 
The 2018’s strategies (promotions, customer acquisition tactics, product portfolio decisions) should be revisited and potentially replicated.

•	Average period of shipment is 3 days, indicating a generally fast logistics. 
There is a streamlined fulfillment in terms of efficient processing, packing, and courier dispatch contributing to customer satisfaction and repeat orders.

•	The customer that contributed the most to profitability was Cascade Grovers and had both the highest product orders, high revenue and a lower-than-average shipping period, making them the ideal target for retention programs and overall service strategies.

•	Overall shipping performance experienced a slight decline from 2017 to 2018, followed by a significant drop between 2018 and 2019 most likely influenced by the significant growth in orders, before showing a modest improvement from 2019 to 2020. The slight recovery in 2020 indicates corrective measures such as better inventory management and order prioritization were implemented though not fully addressed. This trend indicates that shipping efficiency decreased during the peak growth period but began to recover as operational improvements were applied.
